About Me

I'm Ji Zhou, a UX researcher and designer passionate about creating user-centered designs and solutions. With a background in psychology and previous experience in academia, I bring multiple perspectives that enable me to empathize with users and develop effective user-centered solutions.

Biography

Before the age of 30, I achieved my goal of becoming an Assistant Professor in China's top psychology department. However, due to changes in my family plan, I began a new journey in Germany.
Despite working on data projects such as PISA and NEPS and publishing over 20 peer-reviewed journal papers (google h-index 11), balancing life as a mother of two and striving for a permanent position in Germany was still challenging. This led me to a career as a UX professional with Huawei and BMW. Throughout this journey, I've learned invaluable lessons and experienced things I wouldn't have seen had I taken a different path.
